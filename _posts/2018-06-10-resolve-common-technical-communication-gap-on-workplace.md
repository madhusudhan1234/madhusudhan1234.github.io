To perform the work we first set the goal and start to accomplish that goal. The team is working on a common goal but Sometimes gap in communication happen. Those communication gap increase temperature in the company, It is obvious that sometimes in workplace happen those condition but we can minimize using several ways. 
So we will talk more about that in this article.

**TL:DR: Transparent communication and openness with the team are helping to make more good communication and healthy environment. 
Choose the correct medium of communication at the correct time.**

To resolve the communication gap we can do the following.

#### 1. Self Organize  
The team member should be self-organized and also organization should create an environment to make team member self-organized. 
If there is the self-organized team present in the company boss need not request or give the command to accomplish any work for the company.

#### 2. The written copy of Proof
There are lots of roles in any tech company such as Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team, Product Stakeholders etc. During any decision making do not depend on verbal communication only, you need written copy of proof for that because the team is quite busy with their own work. 
The team can forget own decisions and then questions may be raised at any time if anything goes wrong.

Not only tech field, But every business it is a good idea to confirm any business or technical decision made by the team.

#### 3. Make a checklist for the events
We do not have to forget to communicate with the team so do not miss any important events at your company. We are human beings and we may forget those events so make a checklist.
That really helps for us to organize our meetings and daily plans.

#### 4. Open to all
Our team expects we will complete our work on estimated time, But we may face some sort of issues or problems that do not give to complete our work on time. Nobody knows about your situation if you do not tell them then communication gap happens. So we have to feel free to openly tell the team about the situation.

If we are even did something wrong and you know about that then take your responsibility and say sorry for the team. We should be transparent and open in such a way that every other team member should know which work we are doing now.

**Conclusion:** Above are the some solutions to remove the communication gap in workplace. There are some more things may present to remove the communication gap in workplace so please share in comment section. Thanks for Reading, Cheers!
