### 1) How did you get into the Tech?
The funny thing is that I had completed my Bsc. CSIT but I had no any skills related to Information Technology. My initial startup was first I join Computer Institute to learn php. Very next day I change my mind and switch to designing because that is what I love to do.
I used to attend technical programs to keep myself updated with new technologies. My good friend Mr. Madhu Sudhan Subedi helped me on git/github profile to makeup my public portfolio. I got connected with iconic technical people in Linkedin and Twitter. I spent my spare times on research based on designing and I also wrote blogs on medium. This is how I got involved into the technical field.

### 2) Can you explain the importance of Better UI Design in today’s Web Applications?
UI/UX is a brain of Web Design System. We need to understand it. We need to understand the basic frame and position of the sections. Nobody has guts to swap header and footer section. Container wrapper must be there to maintain standard. Typography standard is important. The main objective is website should be as simple as possible and user friendly and it should be understandable by any literate user. Basic example is google. Try google.com there will be no any other option but to just type and search. That’s the point. And most importantly know the difference between what you wanna keep and what you need to keep.

### 3) How do you write clean and maintainable and reusable code so that Backend developer feel comfortable with your code?
As far as becoming a professional web designer, we need to write clean and clear code. There shouldn’t be any flaws. I maintain my code standard by writing a code for particular section and i won’t start code for another section until i finish my current section. After i finish my design i do css validation, i manage my responsive design in seperate sheet. And in case of keeping my code comfortable with backend design, i prefer to use bootstrap, the enhancement of css framework. It is comfortable for both frontend developer and backend developers. There are some utility css with relevant class. Sometimes i prepare basic utility css with relevant class name which makes sense. Eg: If a backend developer need a button then he just need to add class button in the required tag. I hope you got my point.

### 4) What are the things that motivate most to do day-to-day web designing work?
I am not always energetic for my daily work. Sometimes I just don’t get up from my bed. I just open my laptop and surf all day long whatever i like. Sometimes just like to go out for coffee. I often travel outside valley and love to surrender to nature. But most of the time i’m working, all the new frameworks, recent advanced latest and greatest techniques which are far more efficient and easier to use motivates me to do day-to-day web designing work. Everyone hates monotonous work life. We need to charge our body and feed our soul.

### 5) What are your professional goals going forward?
My professional goal is to form a IT Community in my Society and be the helping hand to the fresh graduates who need helps. Maybe I’ll start an Institute for them. Also, i’ll prepare basic IT course and presentation for semi-literate people inside my society to teach them basic knowledge about modern information technology. But I may or may not fulfill my all the goals.

### 6) How do you keep yourself updated?
When we work in IT Company, we get updated in Technology passively. I don’t frequently read technical blogs and articles. But I enjoy spending hours in youtube watching the latest cutting edge technologies. I do attend technical programs and meetup. So I haven’t keep myself technical updated actively very much.
